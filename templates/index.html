Francisco & Iuliana

To continue with our love story, we have decided to celebrate the journey we have walked and to celebrate the one ahead.

Our story

We met in a coastal town, where the sea and the sun blend into an endless horizon.

We both shared a deep connection with the ocean: I would lose myself in the waves while he captured the beauty of the landscape with his camera.

It was in that serene, natural setting where our paths crossed, and since then, the sea has been our special place.

Over time, we decided to start a new adventure together and move to Barcelona, a vibrant city full of energy, close to the sea we both love.

Here, surrounded by the Mediterranean breeze and sunshine, we took the next step in our journey, knowing that the love we share, like the waves, will never stop growing.
